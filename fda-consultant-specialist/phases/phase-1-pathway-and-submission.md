# Phase 1: Pathway and Submission

Load `../references/fda_submission_guide.md` before running this phase.

## Goal

Choose the right FDA pathway and define the submission plan.

## Ask

- What is the device and intended use?
- Is there a plausible predicate?
- Are technological differences likely to undermine substantial
  equivalence?
- Is the risk low/moderate enough for De Novo, or clearly Class III?

## Decide

- 510(k), De Novo, or PMA
- special vs traditional vs abbreviated 510(k), if applicable
- whether a Pre-Sub / Q-Sub would de-risk the plan

Use `scripts/fda_submission_tracker.py` when milestone tracking is
helpful.

## Output

Pathway recommendation with rationale, required sections, key risks, and
submission milestones.
