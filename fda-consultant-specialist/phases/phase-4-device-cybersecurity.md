# Phase 4: Device Cybersecurity

Load `../references/device_cybersecurity_guidance.md` before running this
phase.

## Goal

Assess premarket and postmarket cybersecurity expectations for the
device.

## Ask

- Is the device network-connected?
- Could a cyber incident create patient harm?
- What threat model, SBOM, and testing evidence exist?
- How will vulnerabilities be disclosed, patched, and communicated?

## Cover

- Tier / risk implications
- threat modeling
- authentication and encryption expectations
- SBOM expectations
- penetration testing and vulnerability management
- coordinated vulnerability disclosure

## Output

Cybersecurity plan with submission expectations, postmarket obligations,
and evidence gaps.
