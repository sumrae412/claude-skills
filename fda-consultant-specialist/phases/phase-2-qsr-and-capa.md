# Phase 2: QSR and CAPA

Load `../references/qsr_compliance_requirements.md` before running this
phase. Load `../references/fda_capa_requirements.md` when CAPA depth is
the focus.

## Goal

Assess QSR / 21 CFR 820 readiness, especially design controls and CAPA.

## Ask

- What stage is the device in: design, validation, transfer, or postmarket?
- Which QSR subsystems are already documented?
- Where are design inputs, outputs, reviews, verification, and validation
  recorded?
- How is CAPA initiated, investigated, verified, and closed?

Use `scripts/qsr_compliance_checker.py` when structured assessment helps.

## Output

QSR readiness review with subsystem gaps, CAPA weaknesses, and priority
actions.
