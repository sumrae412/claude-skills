business model.

## Discovery Questions

When the ICP is drafted, generate 5 customer discovery questions that ask about past behavior, never hypothetical intent.

Good (past behavior):

- "What did you do the last time this happened?"
- "How much time or money did it cost you?"
- "What tools or manual workaround do you use now?"

Bad (hypothetical, leading):

- "Would you use this?"
- "Would you pay for this?"
- "Do you like this idea?"

Rule: if a question can be answered with a polite "yes," rewrite it.

## Output

Customer profile with framework labels, 5 past-behavior discovery questions, and strategist's note.
