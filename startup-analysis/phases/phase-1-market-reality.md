# Phase 1: Market Reality

## Goal

Produce a cold market read with TAM, saturation, buyer behavior, pricing
ceiling, channel viability, and kill criteria.

## Cover

- TAM and reasoning
- market maturity and saturation
- dominant players
- buyer sophistication
- purchase frequency
- viable acquisition channels
- CAC / CLV logic when possible
- strategic opportunity mode
- difficulty and verdict

## Output

Market reality table plus kill criteria and strategist's note.
