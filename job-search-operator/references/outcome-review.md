# Outcome review

Review outcomes weekly, after an interview, or after a meaningful batch of decisions. Do not manufacture lessons from tiny samples.

## Funnel

Count distinct roles at each verified stage:

1. pursued
2. applied
3. human contact
4. recruiter screen
5. hiring-manager interview
6. later interview
7. offer

Keep `unknown` separate. Do not upgrade acknowledgment emails into human contact.

## Segment only when useful

Compare outcomes by:

- fit band
- application route: cold, warm, recruiter-led
- role family and level
- source
- résumé positioning angle
- cover letter used or not

Do not claim causation. A segment needs enough cases and a plausible mechanism before it changes strategy.

## Output

Produce:

- **Observed:** verified counts and rates.
- **Pattern:** repeated evidence, if any.
- **Hypothesis:** a possible explanation.
- **Experiment:** one bounded change for the next 5-10 suitable roles.
- **Stop rule:** what result would end or reverse the experiment.

Prefer changing targeting, entry path, or evidence quality before polishing prose again. If applications repeatedly die before human contact, examine fit calibration, credible signals, and warm paths first.
