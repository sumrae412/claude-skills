# Approval contract

## No approval needed

- Read local job-search files.
- Screen user-supplied roles.
- Research public company and role information.
- Draft materials or messages clearly labeled as drafts.
- Update the tracker from an explicit user-reported event.
- Recommend a next action.

## Explicit approval required

- Send email, LinkedIn messages, forms, or referral requests.
- Submit, withdraw, or modify an application on an external site.
- Connect or disconnect email, social, calendar, or job-board accounts.
- Restructure an existing tracker or change historical facts.
- Create scheduled scouting, monitoring, or follow-up automation.

Approval is scoped to the named action and opportunity. “Apply to Acme” does not authorize another company, later follow-ups, or account connections.

Before an outward action, show:

- exact recipient or employer
- exact content or materials
- account or channel used
- irreversible consequences
- final confirmation request

After an approved action, record only verified results. A browser click is not proof of submission; capture the confirmation page, email, or application identifier.
