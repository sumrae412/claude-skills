# Phase 4: German BDSG

Load `../references/german_bdsg_requirements.md` before running this
phase.

## Goal

Assess German-specific requirements that sit on top of GDPR.

## Ask

- Is a DPO required?
- Is employee data processing in scope?
- Is video surveillance involved?
- Are scoring or other Germany-specific regulated practices used?

## Check For

- DPO appointment threshold
- employee-data legal basis
- works council implications
- signage / necessity / retention for video
- authority-registration or local obligations

## Output

German BDSG applicability review with required actions and priority gaps.
