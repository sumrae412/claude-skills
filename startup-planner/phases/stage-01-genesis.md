# Stage 1: Genesis Question

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Identify which simple question drove the product's creation.

## Ask

- Which of these best describes how the idea started?
- What was the specific moment or realization that sparked it?

Use these genesis patterns:

- Therefore, what?
- Isn't this interesting?
- Is there a better way?
- Why doesn't our company do this?
- It's possible, so why don't we?
- Where is the market leader weak?

## Push

- Ask for the concrete trigger, not a polished retrospective story.
- Distinguish personal frustration from real market pull.

## Output

One paragraph capturing the genesis story and the originating question.
