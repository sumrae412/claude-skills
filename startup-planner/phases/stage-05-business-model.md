# Stage 5: Business Model

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Answer who pays and how money reaches the business.

## Ask

- Who exactly is writing the check?
- What price point makes this a no-brainer?
- Can you describe the business model in 10 words or fewer?

Use the Kawasaki model families as prompts:

- SaaS/Freemium
- Printer and Toner
- Market Leader
- Switchboard
- Eyeballs
- Craftsman

## Push

- Force one concrete initial payer.
- Keep the first niche narrow.
- Treat pricing as a hypothesis, not a declaration.

## Output

Business model in 10 words or fewer plus a pricing hypothesis.
