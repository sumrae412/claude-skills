# Stage 2: Sweet Spot Analysis

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Map the overlap between expertise, opportunity, and passion.

## Ask

### Expertise

- What technical skills do you bring?
- What domain knowledge do you have?
- What is missing that you would need to hire for?

### Opportunity

- Who would pay for this today?
- How large is the target market?
- What evidence suggests the opportunity exists?

### Passion

- What about this problem keeps you working on it?
- Could you work on this for 10 years?

## Push

- Separate real capability from aspiration.
- Treat market size claims as hypotheses until validated.
- If only one circle is strong, call that out directly.

## Output

Three bullet lists plus an assessment of whether the overlap is strong
enough to proceed.

## Optional Add-On

If the opportunity feels contested or emotionally loaded, load
`add-ons.md` and consider PRO/CON Debate Validation.
