# Stage 11: Go / NoGo Decision

Load `../references/business-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Make a structured decision to proceed, pivot, or stop.

## Review

If prior decisions exist, extract what worked, what failed, and what
assumptions were wrong.

## Ask

Evaluate:

- Market validation
- Value proposition clarity
- Feasibility with current resources
- Strategic alignment
- Risk level
- Competitive advantage
- Projected ROI

Run a pre-mortem:

`Imagine this failed in 12 months. What was the most likely cause?`

## Push

- Call out confirmation bias, sunk-cost fallacy, and optimism bias.
- Separate reversible risks from existential ones.

## Output

Decision document with rationale, risk register, next steps, and review
date.
