# Stage 4: Make Mantra

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Create a 3-4 word mantra, not a mission statement.

## Explain

- Mission statements are long, abstract, and self-important.
- Mantras are short, memorable, and customer-facing.

## Ask

1. Generate 5-10 candidate mantras.
2. Apply the receptionist test.
3. Apply the opposite test.
4. Narrow to the best 1-3.

## Push

- Remove jargon.
- Keep it positive and outward-facing.
- If it sounds like a company bio, it is wrong.

## Output

Final mantra plus 2-3 runners-up.
