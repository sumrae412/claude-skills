# Stage 9: Market Analysis and Competitive Positioning

Load `../references/business-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Size the market, analyze competitors, and define 2-3 customer personas.

## Ask

### TAM / SAM / SOM

- What evidence can support market size?
- What is a realistic first-year target?

Use web search to validate claims with current sources.

### Competitive Analysis

For each direct or indirect competitor:

- Offering
- Pricing
- Positioning
- Strengths
- Weaknesses
- Customer sentiment

### Personas

Create 2-3 personas with:

- Background
- Goals
- Pain points
- Buying behavior
- Decision criteria

## Push

- Reject market-size handwaving.
- Distinguish competitors from adjacent substitutes.
- Surface red flags: weak need, saturated market, unclear wedge.

## Output

TAM/SAM/SOM estimate, positioning map, and 2-3 personas.
