# Stage 3: Make Meaning

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Define how the product makes the world better.

## Ask

Complete this sentence:

`If [product] never existed, the world would be worse off because _____.`

## Push

Challenge weak answers such as:

- "It saves time."
- "It makes people more efficient."
- "It uses AI to help teams."

Keep pushing until the answer connects to a real human or business
outcome.

## Output

One sentence stating the product's meaning.
