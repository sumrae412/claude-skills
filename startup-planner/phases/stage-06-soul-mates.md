# Stage 6: Soul Mates Assessment

Load `../references/kawasaki-frameworks.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Evaluate cofounder needs and team gaps.

## Ask

- Are you building this solo or with cofounders?
- Which roles are already covered?
- What is the biggest missing role?
- Would a cofounder solve a real execution gap or only signal
  fundability?

Use the Kawasaki lens:

- Similarities needed: vision, size ambition, commitment level
- Differences needed: expertise, orientation, perspective

## Push

- Do not treat "need a cofounder" as automatic.
- Distinguish what can be hired from what must be co-owned.

## Output

Current team assessment plus the highest-priority cofounder or hiring gap.
