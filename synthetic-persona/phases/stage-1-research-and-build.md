# Stage 1: Research and Build Persona

Load `../references/research-guide.md` before running this stage.

## Goal

Collect public data and build a reusable persona card plus persona prompt.

## Ask

- Who should be modeled?
- What is their role and relationship to the product or decision?
- What public sources already exist?
- Are any personality-framework hints known?

## Build

- gather sources
- organize evidence with empathy-map structure
- extract priorities, style, values, frustrations, mental models, and
  positive reactions
- define JTBD
- synthesize persona card
- generate persona prompt
- save both to memory

## Output

Persona card, persona prompt, and confidence assessment.
