# Phase 1: Scope and TSC Selection

Load `../references/type1_vs_type2.md` and
`../references/trust_service_criteria.md` before running this phase.

## Goal

Define SOC 2 scope, choose Type I or Type II, and select the right Trust
Service Criteria.

## Ask

- What customers or deals are driving this audit?
- Is the goal fast assurance or mature operating evidence?
- What systems, services, and data flows are in scope?
- Which customer commitments depend on uptime, confidentiality, privacy,
  or processing integrity?

## Decide

- Type I vs Type II
- Mandatory Security scope
- Whether Availability, Confidentiality, Processing Integrity, or Privacy
  are justified
- Which subservice organizations matter

## Output

Scope statement with:

- audit type
- in-scope systems
- selected TSC categories
- business rationale
- key exclusions and assumptions
