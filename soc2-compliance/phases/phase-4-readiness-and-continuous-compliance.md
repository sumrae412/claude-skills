# Phase 4: Readiness and Continuous Compliance

Load `../references/type1_vs_type2.md` and
`../references/evidence_collection_guide.md` before running this phase.

## Goal

Assess audit readiness and design the ongoing compliance operating model.

## Ask

- Are controls documented, owned, and operating?
- Is evidence complete for the relevant period?
- What findings recur because compliance is still point-in-time?
- What dashboards, alerts, or recurring reviews would keep the program
  audit-ready?

## Evaluate

Cover:

- policy freshness
- access-review cadence
- vulnerability-management evidence
- incident-response testing
- DR / BCP testing
- vendor reassessment status

## Output

Readiness report with:

- readiness score
- blocking issues
- pre-audit action list
- continuous compliance plan
