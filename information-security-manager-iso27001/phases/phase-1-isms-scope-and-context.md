# Phase 1: ISMS Scope and Context

Load `../references/iso27001-controls.md` before running this phase.

## Goal

Define organizational context, interested parties, and ISMS boundaries.

## Ask

- What business unit, product, or environment is in scope?
- Which legal, regulatory, and customer requirements matter?
- Which systems, teams, and third parties are included?
- What is explicitly out of scope?

## Build

Document:

- scope statement
- interested parties
- internal and external issues
- key dependencies
- management ownership

## Output

ISMS scope and context statement with assumptions, exclusions, and
approval points.
