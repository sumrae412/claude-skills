# Phase 4: Incident Response and Certification Readiness

Load `../references/incident-response.md` and
`../references/iso27001-controls.md` before running this phase.

## Goal

Assess incident-response capability and certification readiness.

## Ask

- How are incidents detected, triaged, contained, and reviewed?
- What metrics prove the ISMS is operating?
- Have internal audit and management review actually happened?
- What evidence exists for training, incidents, and corrective actions?

## Evaluate

Cover:

- incident classification and response timing
- evidence preservation
- recovery and lessons learned
- internal audit status
- management review status
- certification-stage readiness

## Output

Readiness assessment with blocking gaps, evidence requirements, and next
actions before audit.
